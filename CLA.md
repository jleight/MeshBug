## 8. How to sign

You sign this CLA **once** by replying to the CLA bot's comment on your
first pull request with the exact line:

> I have read the MeshBug CLA and agree to its terms for this and all future
> contributions to the Project.

The CLA bot ([contributor-assistant/github-action]
(https://github.com/contributor-assistant/github-action)) runs on every PR.
It records your GitHub username and timestamp in
`signatures/v1/cla.json` on the `cla-signatures` branch of this repository.
After your first signature, all subsequent PRs from the same GitHub account
are auto-recognized and the bot adds a green check without further action.

For entity contributions, also include in the PR description:

> I am authorized to sign on behalf of *Company Name*, and this contribution
> is made on its behalf.

If a future change to this CLA is material, the bot will mark prior
signatures as expired and ask contributors to re-sign on their next PR.
